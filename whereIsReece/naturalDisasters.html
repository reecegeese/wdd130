As the name implies Natural Disasters is the study of Natural Disasters. Class is Mondays, Wednesdays, and Firdays from 10:15 to 11:15. On Monday there is usually a small reading assignment to do, then later in the week on Thursday I do whatever else the week has, usually a share insights or lab assignment. Finally on Friday the last assignment for the week is the disaster report, writing down every significant disaster that happened in the world that week
